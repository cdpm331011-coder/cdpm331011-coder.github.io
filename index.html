About Me

Turning climate
crisis into
classroom action

I'm a climate educator, researcher, and institutional capacity builder working at the intersection of environmental vulnerability and public school systems in Pakistan. My career spans frontline teaching in flood-affected communities, policy advocacy, teacher development, and founding a national climate education initiative.

My doctoral research explores how teachers exercise agency in enacting Climate Change and Sustainability Education within public schools in Pakistan's most flood-vulnerable districts — a question with urgent real-world stakes.

Current Research

"Teacher Agency and the Enactment of Climate Change and Sustainability Education in Flood-Affected Public Schools in Pakistan: A Multiple Case Study of District Rajanpur and Swat."
